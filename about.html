About

The club was formed in 1988, our club philosophy is to create a community of mountain bikers for the promotion and enjoyment of mountain biking in the area of Christchurch. We organise rides locally and around NZ to meet new riding companions and discover new trails. Our club is a fun social group of friends who motivate each other to ride more, improve fitness and riding ability.
